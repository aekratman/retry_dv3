__Hello, participants!__

The premise of this study is simple. You will review three pie charts which concern the local elections of Everywhereville, USA. The percentage each candidate is voted for are all  __randomly generated__. 

Your job is simple: guess by how large a percentage your favorite candidate, __Jane Doe__, won or lost to __Jake Q. Driveway__.

Each pie chart is oriented differently, to study how visualizations effect interpretation of data. Please look over each chart and make your best guess: we are looking for intitial reactions, not a full mathematical investigation.

Thank you for your time!!
